MORE ABOUT TENANTS!

Tenant is a disruptive real-estate platform that makes it possible to buy/sell/rent a house without paying any brokerage.

Each listing is verfied and made sure that these are direct owners or shared accommodation parties and there are no middlemen or brokers.

We use lot of heuristics and techniques to ensure that you get a totally broker free list. We have also tried to ensure that maximum information is available to you in as easy to use format.

This ensures that you get a very good idea of the property even before you visit it.

Thus, you can shortlist flats sitting at the comfort of your home without actually traveling all the good and bad properties. This saves your time and effort and with a quick shortlist of 4-5 properties you can actually get a house in few hours!

If you are a landlord interested in posting your apartments to Tenant, please email us at c@tenant.in and we will get in touch to help you list the property. And tenants, happy hunting and get in touch with us to let us know how else we can help!
